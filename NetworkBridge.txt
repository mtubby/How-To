Setup the bridge
$ nmcli connection add type bridge ifname br0 stp no

add slave
$ nmcli connection add type bridge-slave ifname enp62s0 master br0

#check connections
$ nmcli connection show --active

down the eth0
$ nmcli connection down "Ethernet connection 1"

up the bridge
$ nmcli connection up bridge-br0
$ nmcli connection up bridge-slave-en62s0
